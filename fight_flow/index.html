International Flow to London

This panel shows the trend of international visitors to London from 2002 to 2020. The number of visitors grew steadily over time. Although there was a sharp drop in 2020 due to the COVID-19 pandemic, the numbers began to recover soon after. This highlights London’s strong appeal to global tourists and the important role tourism plays in the city’s economy.
